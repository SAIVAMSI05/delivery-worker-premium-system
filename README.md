### Project Overview ### Intelligent Insurance & Risk Protection System for Delivery Workers
The foundation of contemporary food and package delivery systems is made up of delivery personnel. They do, however, face a number of risks, including car damage, fraudulent orders, traffic accidents, and erratic earnings. The majority of delivery platforms offer inadequate insurance coverage, putting employees at risk financially.



A **Smart Insurance and Risk Protection System** created especially for delivery workers is proposed in this project. To guarantee worker safety and financial stability, the system combines **AI-driven risk detection and fraud monitoring** with a **weekly premium-based protection model**.

Delivery workers can sign up for an inexpensive weekly insurance plan through the platform, which automatically initiates claims under predetermined circumstances.

# Problem Statement
Delivery workers frequently encounter risks during their daily operations, including:

- Road accidents during delivery
- Fraudulent or fake delivery orders
- Vehicle damage or breakdown
- High-risk delivery zones
- Lack of financial protection or insurance

Current delivery platforms rarely provide adequate insurance systems tailored for delivery workers. This project aims to solve that gap by building a **parametric insurance system integrated with AI-driven risk analysis**.

# System Workflow

The application workflow is designed to provide **simple access and automated protection for delivery workers**.

1. Delivery worker registers on the platform
2. Worker selects a suitable weekly premium plan
3. The system calculates premium based on risk parameters
4. Delivery activities are monitored in real time
5. AI models analyze data to detect fraud or risk patterns
6. If a trigger condition occurs, insurance claim is activated
7. Claim verification is processed automatically
8. Compensation is provided to the worker

# Weekly Premium Model

The insurance model is based on a **dynamic weekly premium system** that adjusts according to delivery activity and risk exposure.

### Premium Calculation Factors

- Number of deliveries completed
- Distance traveled per week
- Delivery location risk level
- Accident history
- Working hours

### Example Premium Calculation
Base Premium = ₹100 per week

Risk Area Factor = ₹20
High Delivery Volume = ₹30

Total Weekly Premium = ₹150

This model ensures that the premium remains **affordable while providing meaningful protection**.

---

# Parametric Insurance Triggers

Parametric triggers automatically activate insurance claims when predefined conditions occur.

### Trigger Events

- Sudden GPS stop indicating a possible accident
- Delivery delay exceeding threshold time
- Fraudulent order detection
- Vehicle breakdown during delivery
- High-risk location alerts

Once triggered, the system automatically initiates a claim verification process.
